Famous Tourist Places in Hyderabad

Charminar

Charminar

The Charminar is a monument located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. Constructed in 1591, the landmark is a symbol of Hyderabad and officially incorporated in the emblem of Telangana. An iconic 16th-century mosque and monument in the heart of Hyderabad.

Hussain Sagar Lake

Hussain Sagar Lake

Hussain Sagar alternatively referred to as Tank Bund, is a heart-shaped lake in Hyderabad, Telangana, India, built by Ibrahim Quli Qutb Shah in 1562. A large artificial lake with a giant Buddha statue in the center.A large monolithic statue of the Gautama Buddha, erected in 1992, stands on Gibraltar Rock in the middle of the lake. The lake separates the city centre of Hyderabad from the neighbourhood of Secunderabad.

Salar Jung Museum

Salar Jung Museum

The Salar Jung Museum is an art museum located at Dar-ul-Shifa, on the southern bank of the Musi River in the city of Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It is one of the notable National Museums of India. One of the largest art museums in India, with rare collections from around the world.
